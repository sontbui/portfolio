## Awards & recognition

- **3rd Prize — Scientific Research Competition, Faculty of Information Technology, Ho Chi Minh City Open University (2024).** Son's research project was a **food-ordering mobile app** (supporting both **iOS and Android**) whose standout feature was **AI food recognition built on a CNN using YOLOv8**: photograph a dish — say, a bowl of phở — and the app identifies it by name and suggests the best-rated nearby restaurants serving it. The project combined computer vision, mobile development, and location-based recommendations into one working product, and reflects Son's habit of building end-to-end systems rather than isolated demos.

## Professional AI / NLP experience

Beyond test automation, Son has hands-on professional AI experience: he worked with a small team **building machine-learning models for Natural Language Processing tasks**. This background in training and applying models — both NLP and computer vision (YOLOv8) — is part of why he treats AI as an engineering component he understands from the inside, not a black box he merely calls.

## Engineering impact at OPSWAT (approximate)

- **Automation coverage: 0 → ~80%** on the targeted product.
- **~700 cross-platform automated tests** across Windows, macOS, and Linux.
- **Regression execution time reduced ~70%.**
- **Flaky tests reduced from ~150 to fewer than 20** via detection and quarantine.
- **Manual testing effort reduced ~40%** (estimated).

## AI Automation Platform — proof-of-concept results

- **~20 minutes** from Jira ticket to pull request for a typical task (~10 test cases), measured in POC runs.
- **6 specialized agents** collaborating across the testing lifecycle.
- **End-to-end**: requirement analysis through PR with no manual step in between.

## Beacon — shipped internal product

Son also designed and shipped **Beacon**, a cross-platform Electron desktop platform (an "AI operating system for engineers") used daily, with 278+ versioned internal builds — covered in depth in its own case study.

## How to frame the biggest achievement

The single most significant achievement is architecting the AI-powered automation platform that removes the repetitive engineering between a requirement and its verification — combined with the framework work that took a manual process to ~80% automated coverage with a dramatically more stable suite. His research prize and NLP work show the same pattern earlier in his journey: applied AI, shipped as a working product.
