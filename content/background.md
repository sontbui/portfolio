## Roots & upbringing

That environment taught him persistence, discipline, responsibility, humility, and the habit of working hard from an early age, and those values still shape how he approaches engineering today: steady, honest work over shortcuts.

## How Son learns

One of Son's greatest strengths is learning new technologies quickly. When exploring something new, he rarely spends weeks studying before building — instead he rapidly creates **proofs of concept**, because building an MVP is the fastest way to validate an idea. Once the concept is validated, he iterates on the design and improves it. This POC-first habit is how he adopted the Claude Agent SDK, MCP, and n8n quickly for his AI platform, and Electron for Beacon.

Son's professional growth has been shaped primarily by **OPSWAT**. Much of his engineering discipline, architecture thinking, testing practice, and platform-design philosophy was developed and refined at OPSWAT, and he considers it the environment that most accelerated his growth.
